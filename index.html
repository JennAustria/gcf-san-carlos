Who We Are

A family of faith in the heart of San Carlos

We are an evangelical, Bible-believing church longing to see ordinary people meet an extraordinary God — and become more like Jesus together.

Our Mission. To make disciples of Jesus Christ who love God, love people, and make Him known in San Carlos and beyond.

Our Vision. A growing community of believers, rooted in the Word, empowered by the Spirit, and devoted to one another as a family.

What We Believe. The gospel of Jesus Christ — His life, death, and resurrection — is the power of God for salvation to everyone who believes.
